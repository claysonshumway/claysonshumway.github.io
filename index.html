Bio

I'm an applied microeconomist on the job market in the 2025-26 year. I study environmental topics, focusing on the causes and effects of air pollution and extreme temperature. I'm also interested in studying the effects of trade on environmental outcomes. I have published in the Journal of Public Economics and Stata Journal.
